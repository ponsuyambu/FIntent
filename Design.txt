Design Decisions
============================
1. If a fragment is not added to the backstack, it will not be removed from the fragment manager
even when clear back stack is called. i.e POP_BACK_STACK_INCLUSIVE. It is creating weird problems in fragment life cycle
also. Due to this reasons, in this design, FRAGMENT IS ALWAYS ADDED TO BACKSTACK. BASED ON THE USE CASE, CORRESPONDING FRAGMENTS
WILL BE POPPED. This decision solves most of the complex issue with fragment backstack navigation.
